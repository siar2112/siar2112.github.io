    Market Research

    Nowadays, with the pandemic several platform have been developed and are currently being use to try to meet these elder people's requirements. Here are a a few example:
    • Skype:
      An online meeting platform where people can chat through their webcam together. It is developed by Microsoft since 2003 and it is one the first software that offered face time communication. It offers many great features such as webcam chatting, screen sharing and private messaging. Elder people use it especially for communicating online with their loved ones.
    • Zoom:
      A software perfect for online group session. It started in 2011 and exploded in 2020 during the first quarantine. Most schools are using it. Its greatest features are group sessions. One host can create a meetings and animate it easily. Elder people use it especially for participating in online events
    • MyFitness Pal:
      It's a software that keeps tracks diet and exercises of the user. It was created in 2005. It offers an alimentary journal, recipe tips, conditioning programs and a great community to share experiences with. Aged people that are familiar to technology uses it for their workout.
    • Aaptiv:
      It's a platform that offers thousands of workout programs based on user's goals. It was created in 2021 during the pandemic situations Elder people uses it for sports exercises
