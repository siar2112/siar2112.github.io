    HOW THE VALUE SYSTEM WORKS

    • Players receive 5M $ for winning a game, 3M $ for drawing and 1M $ for loosing.
    • They loose 6M $ when they receive a red card and 3M $ a yellow card.
    • Before each competitions, they can decide to play as one the following position when they register. They must choose wisely as each position give them a bonus differently every game:

    • DEFENDER:

      A defender want his team to get the less goals possible. As so, their bonus is calculated by 6M $ - (the number of goals their team receives). They also get 2M $ per goal they score

    • ATTACKER:

      An attacker want his team to score the most goals possible. They receives 4M $ per goal they score

    • GOALIE:

      Goalkeepers must protect their net att all cost. Their bonus is calculated by 8M $ - (the number of goals their team receives). They don't receives bonus for scoring a goal. There can be maximum 2 goalkeepers in a team
