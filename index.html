    INTRODUCTION

    The Covid pandemic affected the mental and physical health of every quebecers. There are no deny that the most touched population are our precious elderly people. Not only they are the most vulnerable to the virus, but also most of them are completly isolated inside a small room. An american study found that elder people that lived in a CHSLD with strict measures had more chance to die from causes not related to Covid. They get depressed and give up on their will to live. The need for technology to support their mental and physical is bigger than ever

    PROBLEM

    Our elderly people are getting more depressed than ever with this pandemic. They must follow strict measures to isolate themselves. Therefore, their resources to socialize and exercise have been significantly diminished

    SOLUTION

    Our team will build a platform where the elderly will be assigned to small online classrooms with specialized trainers who will host either a conditioning workout or a social gaming session. The users will have at least one class a day where they will be expected to attend. Our team will design the UI so it would be easy to use for them. Our platform will especially target those who are isolated in a CHSLD
